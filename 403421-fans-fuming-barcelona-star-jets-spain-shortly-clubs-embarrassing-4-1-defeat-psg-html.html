    Griezzman risks anger from fans after jetting out of Barcelona after PSG mauling

    - Antoine Griezmann was in action when PSG humiliated Barcelona 4-1 right at the Camp Nou - The Frenchman immediately flew to Lyon after the game, leaving club supporters furious - Manager Koeman has granted the team two days off until they resume training again on Friday

    French forward Antoine Griezmann made a trip to Lyon immediately after his side Barcelona were handed a 4-1 humiliating defeat in the first leg round of 16 Champions League tie by PSG, Mirror reports.

    Reports have it that some Barcelona fans are upset with the player by his decision to make the trip to France after they were beaten.

    Impressive Paris Saint-Germain handed Barcelona a stunning defeat right at the Camp Nou as the UEFA Champions League action returned on Tuesday night, February 16.

    Kylian Mbappe netted a hat-trick in the encounter as well as a Moise Kean goal for the visitors, despite captain Lionel Messi had put Barcelona ahead early in the game.

    Their manager Ronald Koeman has granted the team two days off until they resume training again on Friday.

    And considering his largely ineffective performance, Griezmann was the target of fury from some on social media.

    One fan tweeted: "We lose 4-1 to PSG and Griezmann gets a private plane to go on holiday for a couple of days?"

    Also, an on-field incident occurred when it was 1-1 as former Manchester United defender Pique was visibly upset with the team and then had a heated argument with Griezmann.

    It happened at a time Barcelona were defending a corner as the full heated-argument was caught by pitch-side microphones.
