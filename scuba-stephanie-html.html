    Instagram star and cosplay artist known for transforming into characters inspired by films like A Nightmare on Elm Street, Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery, and Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone. She also dressed as characters from video games like Super Mario and anime series like Attack on Titan. She has earned more than 550,000 followers on her scuba.steph account.

    Before Fame

    She joined Twitter in January 2018. 

    Trivia

    She has been inspired by superheroes such as Spider-Man and Catwoman. She also dressed as the McDonald's mascot Ronald McDonald. 

    Family Life

    She is from the United States. 

    Associated With

    She dressed as Poison Ivy, the DC character played by actresses Uma Thurman, Maggie Geha, and Peyton List.
