    Nigerian crossdresser, James Brown has reacted to Bobrisky  being chased out of Benin.

    Recall that Naija News earlier reported that the Bobrisky was thrown out of Benin after he excitedly made a video asking the Oba to come and marry him shortly after his arrival.

    The video did not sit well with the indigenes of the state and they had allegedly ‘thrown him out’ despite a public apology.

    James Brown, who is known to be a sworn enemy of Bob, has mocked him for being chased out by the indigenes.

    He stated that he visited Benin and had no clashes with anyone because he respected their culture.

    James asked Bob to visit him so he could teach him how to respect people’s culture.
