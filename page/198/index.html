Zamfara PDP Governorship Primary Election Nullified

01 Jan 0001, 00:00

The Zamfara State Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) governorship primary election conducted in May 2022 has been declared null. A ruling by the Federal High Court sitting in Gusau, on Friday cancelled the processes that produced Dauda Lawan Dare as the party’s flag-bearer for the 2023 general elections, Naija News reports. The court ruling came to be following a prayer by the counsel plaintiffs, Barrister Ibrahim Aliyu. Presiding over the suit on Friday (today), the court judge, Justice Aminu Bappa explained that the court granted all the prayers of the plaintiffs and cancelled the election of Dauda Lawan Dare as the party’s flag bearer for the 2023 general elections.
