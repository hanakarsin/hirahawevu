Ogun PDP Guber Candidate, Ladi Adebutu Arraigned In Court

02 Apr 2024, 00:00

The candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in the last governorship election in Ogun State, Ladi Adebutu, was arraigned before the State High Court in Abeokuta on Tuesday. Naija News reports that Adebutu and nine others were arraigned by the federal government over alleged money laundering. However, at the court sitting today, the government made changes to the charges filed against the PDP chieftain and the other defendants. These amendments were made in order to include Adebutu in the ongoing case that was initiated in July 2023, which accuses him of financially influencing voters during the March 18 election.
