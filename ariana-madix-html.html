    Featured on MTV, CollegeHumor.com, and New York Magazine, she is a television and web actress who has also been seen on ESPN.com's series NFL Writer's Room.

    Before Fame

    She was an award-winning Equestrian rider and also a national champion in cheerleading, pom-jazz dance, and hip hop dance.

    Trivia

    She graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in theatre and broadcasting from Flagler College and later studied at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts.

    Family Life

    She was born in Melbourne, Florida and moved to New York City to pursue her career in entertainment. She has a brother named Jeremy. She frequently posts photos on Instagram with her reality star boyfriend Tom Sandoval.

    Associated With

    She has been featured on the reality series Vanderpump Rules alongside Tom Sandoval, whom she would begin dating.
