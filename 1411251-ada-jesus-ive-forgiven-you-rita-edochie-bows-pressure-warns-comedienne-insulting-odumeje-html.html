    Ada Jesus, I've Forgiven You: Rita Edochie Bows to Pressure, Warns Comedienne against Insulting Odum

    - Edochie, however, strongly warned Ada Jesus against insulting men of God, particularly Prophet Odumeje

    Veteran Nollywood actress, Rita Edochie, has had a change of mind hours after vowing to never forgive ailing comedienne, Ada Jesus.

    Recall that Legit.ng previously gathered the report of a viral video showing the moment the actress and Prophet Chukwuemeke Odumeje vowed to never forgive the young lady during a church service programme.

    Odumeje and Edochie had claimed that the comedienne went around soiling their names and accusing them of performing fake miracles in their church.

    The two had also taken turns to shout down on the immobile comedian as they noted that she would never heal from the sickness.

    However, hours after the video went viral in the online community, Edochie has now returned with a video noting that she has heeded the plea of fans around the world to forgive the young lady.

    The actress wrote:

    "I am happy that the WHOLE WORLD has seen that Ada Jesus framed all SORTS of LIES and HEAPED on ME. I have been VINDICATED by Ada's PREDICAMENTS. The good LORD has FOUGHT my BATTLE for me. As a RESULT of PLEADINGS from people, PARTICULARLY my FANS, i have FORGIVEN her. Let HER beg GOD for FORGIVENESS and HEALING."

    Edochie went on to advise the young lady and others like her against speaking badly about men of God, particularly Prophet Odumeje.
