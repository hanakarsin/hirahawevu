    Many are the times parents face many challenges while trying to raise their children the right way.

    It is even harder for working parents who have to go to work but still find time to play their roles as parents.

    Former Auntie Boss actress Nyce Wanjeri is not an exception in this case owing to the fact that she is a mother to a cute little girl.

    The actress may be a busy mum who is always out trying to create content but also spares equal time to spend with her family.

    Well, this, she confirmed recently after sharing a photo of herself spending quality time with her daughter during a photoshoot.

    Nyce, on Tuesday, June 15, shared a photo of herself rocking a goldish white dress and a hairstyle to match it all.

    Next to her was her daughter who also rocked a white dress almost similar to her mum's.

    Looking at the photo, one could tell the two share quite an impressive bond.

    The actress could not just spam her fans with photos without sharing some wisdom nuggets with them.

    In the caption, Nyce took her time to advise parents on how they can enjoy a positive relationship with their children.

    Nyce noted that parents should also listen to their children and not ignore them whenever they want to share.
