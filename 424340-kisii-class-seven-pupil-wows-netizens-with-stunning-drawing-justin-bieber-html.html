    A class seven pupil has left many impressed after her amazing drawing of the celebrated Canadian singer Justin Drew Bieber was shared on social media.

    The pupil, Daniela Kerubu schools at St Mary's Tabaka Girls Primary School located in South Mugirango Constituency, Kisii county.

    As seen in the almost perfect drawing shared by her tutor, Arthur Bells, on Tuesday, August 24, Daniela captured the singer's signature bowl cut and his earings.

    Speaking to TUKO.co.ke, Bells said the 13-year-old student who loves Bieber, has a passion for drawing and spends most of her free time sketching.

    "The student has a passion in drawing, she has a drawing book and likes sketching photos. She loves nature and enjoys turning her imagination into reality," said Bells.

    The teacher added that for the two years he has been her class teacher, he has been mentoring her to perfect her drawing, singing and dancing talents.

    Despite being multitalented, Bells disclosed that Daniela was a bright student. He said she aspires to be a pilot and a cartoonist.

    DJ Khalid drawing

    In other news, a Kenyan artist impressed many on social media with his drawing of renowned DJ and music producer DJ Khaled.

    The artist known as @Duncoarts on Twitter shared an impressive drawing of the US star with two pictures, one showing the art partly done and the final work.

    The portrait showed the entertainer with an eagle resting on his shoulder while holding a golden key with its beak.

    The artist picked the idea from the poster cover of Khaled's recent single featuring Drake dubbed Popstar.
