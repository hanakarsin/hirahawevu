    12 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A video making the rounds on social media has shown the moment Wizkid confirmed that he has an upcoming album with someone people feel he is not friends with.

    In the short clip, the singer explained that people often assumed that he is not in good terms with the yet-to-be identified singer because he has never done a song with him.

    He, however, announced that contrary to what people think they are actually good friends and have done a collabo together that will soon be released.

    In his words: “So, I have two albums ready, I have one collab, somebody people said I’ve never made a song with because they think we’re not friends. We’re very close friends, he’s on my album too. It’s gonna be something very special and exciting for Africa…”

    Reacting to the video, netizens are of the opinion that he is referring to Davido as there have often being speculations that there is a rift between the duo.
