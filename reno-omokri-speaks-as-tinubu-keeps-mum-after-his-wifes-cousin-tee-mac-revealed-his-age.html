    Reno Omokri Speaks As Tinubu Keeps Mum After His Wifes Cousin, Tee Mac Revealed His Age

    Naija News earlier reported that Tee Mac claimed that Tinubu is 86 years old and warned Nigerians against voting for him in 2023. According to the renowned flutist who said Tinubu is his in-law, the APC presidential candidate is not fit to rule Nigeria.

    15 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Former presidential aide, Reno Omokri, has criticized the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu, for keeping silent after his wife’s cousin, Tee Mac revealed his alleged real age.

    Naija News earlier reported that Tee Mac claimed that Tinubu is 86 years old and warned Nigerians against voting for him in 2023.

    According to the renowned flutist who said Tinubu is his in-law, the APC presidential candidate is not fit to rule Nigeria.

    He condemned Tinubu for not revealing his real age, source of wealth and background despite his agenda to rule Nigeria.

    However, Reno Omokri, in a post shared on Instagram mocked Tinubu for not 1being able to debunk the claims.

    He noted that the APC flag bearer lived with Tee Mac’s stepfather, which is the reason he cannot produce any birth certificate or photos with his classmate because it will expose the lies about his age.

    The political activist further urged the former Governor of Lagos State to be proud of his age as not everyone gets to live that long.

    He said: “48 hours after Tee-Mac Omatsola, his in-law (his wife’s cousin) said Tinubu is 86, Tinubu has not denied it. Tinubu lived in Tee-Mac’s stepfather’s house (Mobolaji Bank Anthony). He has photos. Tee-Mac is 74. He used to call Tinubu ‘uncle’. Yet, Tinubu says he is 70? How can? That is why Tinubu cannot produce any birth certificates or photos with his classmates. Because it will expose the lies about his age.

    “Tinubu should be proud of his age. Not everybody gets to live that long. How and why should you be ashamed of your real age? Mahathir Mohamad was Prime Minister of Malaysia at 95. Embrace your age of 86. How can you be younger than Tee Mac, who is 74 and called you ‘uncle’ in his youth, when you lived in his stepfather’s house.”
