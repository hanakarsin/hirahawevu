    Isreal DMW, the logistics manager to Nigerian singer, Davido, has left celebrity chef, Chioma Avril Rowland, smiling as he complimented her exceptional cooking skills.

    I Once Had S3x On Third Mainland Bridge

    Meanwhile, popular music producer, Collins Micheal Ajereh also known as Don Jazzy, has disclosed the most spontaneous thing he has ever done.

    The Mavin record boss disclosed this when he was asked the most spontaneous thing he had ever done.

    The star maker further revealed that if given a chance to exchange selves with anyone for 24 hours, it would be Elon Musk; the world’s richest man.

    Don Jazzy also disclosed that he likes washing plates.
