    Lady Stuns Internet with 'Humble' Room Transformation, Photos Inspire Many

    In appreciation of her steady progress, a lady lighted social media after she showcased the different transformation her room underwent.

    In a Twitter post, the lady with the username @marothi_vincent posted the previous looks of her room, at a time when it was poorly furnished and went on to share other frames capturing the transformed look as furniture and fitting were fixed.

    @marothi_vincent captioned it:

    "We all start somewhere, and we donʼt stop."

    Her tweet gained huge traction, with over 11k likes and multiple comments as tweeps found the photos and the story behind them to be inspiring.

    Others took the opportunity to recount their shelter challenges and how they overcame them.

    TUKO.co.ke sampled a few:

    @KwenaSidney wrote:

    "That’s what I’m going through right now but hopefully I will not give up God is by my side one day it shall make sense. I really have a painful story to tell. Anyway is life I did not know that I’m this strong by now I thought I should have died."

    @MzayiyaKhanya said:

    "I moved from place to place... but that was not the point, the point was we don't stop, we keep improving."

    @zenwafs commented:

    "My kinda thing. So long as i have a mattress to sleep and a two plate to cook kemonate."

    @CubzelerMkhize remarked:

    "You remind me of the time when I moved to Port Shepstone I hand my bagfull of clothes and i got to NPS I bought a smallnyana mattress, cattle , iron, 25l bucket and basin. After 1.5 year I had to hire a truck to get my things. Progress is not rushed."

    In a separate story, a young lady excited netizens after she shared her passion for farming.

    Identified as Maria Egwakhide, the lady said she had chosen to make good use of her break from school to venture into her passion.

    She shared photos of her large tract of land where she cultivates cassavas.

    She was lauded for her efforts s she proved that women could also outdo themselves in such fields as farming.
