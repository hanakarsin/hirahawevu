    Pastor Ismael Denis Omondi allegedly forged the constitution and meeting minutes of a rival church to divert funds to his church.

    Appearing before Kwale Senior Principal Magistrate Joe Omido, Omondi was accused of forging Service Ministries' constitution to divert its funds to Restoration Kenya Ministries.

    As reported by The Standard, the man of God was further accused of forging minutes of the Annual General Meeting to the Registrar of Societies.
