    Opened officially in 1984 by His Excellency the late President Mwai Kibaki, Mathenge Technical Training Institute is among the famous public tertiary institutions in the country. It is one of the fastest-growing scientific and technological institutions in the country, attracting students from all over Kenya. Here is all you should know about Mathenge Technical Training Institute courses, fees, application process, and contacts.

    TABLE OF CONTENTS

    The institute is approximately 12 kilometres from Othaya town and 18 kilometres from Nyeri town. It also operates under the Board of Governors (BOG), with its principal serving as the Chief Executive Officer and the BOG's secretary. As a competent institute in the region, Mathenge TTI sticks to its core values and mission, a reason why most form leavers and those looking to acquire technical skills now want to join it.

    Mathenge Technical Training Institute courses

    With over three decades of existence, Mathenge Technical Training Institute has many courses that cut across all aspects of life. Its courses are divided into eight departments, all with a primary target to equip students with innovative skills for all-round excellence. Here is a comprehensive list of departments and Mathenge technical courses offered.

    1. Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

    The department offers seven courses: diploma, certificate, and artisan. They include:

    • Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Power Option)
    • Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Telecommunication Option)
    • Certificate in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Power Option)
    • Certificate in Electronics and Electronics Engineering (Telecommunication Option)
    • Artisan in Electrical Installation
    • Electrical Wireman
    • Solar Photo Voltaic

    2. Department of Agriculture

    The department has only three courses, including:

    • Diploma in General Agriculture
    • Certificate in General Agriculture
    • Artisan in General Agriculture

    3. Department of Hospitality and Tourism

    This department aims to equip students with the best culinary and service skills. These allow them to work in local, national, and international hotel settings. Some of the diploma and certificate courses include:

    • Diploma in Supply Chain Management
    • Diploma in Sales and Marketing
    • Diploma in Cooperative Management
    • Certificate in Community and Social Work
    • Certificate in Sales and Marketing
    • Certificate in Business Management

    4. Department of Automotive Engineering

    Automotive engineering was among the initial departments and focused on craft I, II, and III certificates. However, after numerous considerations, it was replaced with the Technical Education Program (TEP) in 1990 and finally with the modular program in 2011. Today, the department has craft, artisan, and diploma courses. These include:

    • Diploma in Automotive Engineering
    • Craft in Welding and Fabrication
    • Artisan in Motor Vehicle Mechanics
    • Motor Vehicle Mechanics
    • Welding and Fabrication

    5. Department of Building and Civil Engineering

    Building and Civil Engineering is the second largest department after the School of Business. The department has various courses. The programs are also examined by the Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC).

    • Diploma in Civil Engineering
    • Diploma in Water Technology
    • Certificate in Sales and Marketing
    • Craft in Building Technology
    • Artisan in Plumbing and Water Services
    • Plumber Piper Fitter
    • Masonry

    6. Department of Fashion Design and Clothing Technology

    • Diploma in Fashion Design and Clothing Technology
    • Craft in Fashion Design and Clothing Technology

    • Artisan in Hairdressing and Beauty Therapy

    7. Department of Information and Communication Technology

    The department equips students with advanced skills to manoeuvre the diverse information and communication space. The two primary courses offered are:

    • Diploma in Information Communication Technology
    • Certificate in Information Communication Technology

    8. Department of Business Studies

    The School of Business is probably the largest institution. It was established in 1988 with 21 students who took Accounts Clerk National Certificate (ACNC) courses. Today, the department has more than ten diploma and certificate programs, including:

    • Diploma in Supply Chain Management
    • Diploma in Sales and Marketing
    • Diploma in Human Resource Management
    • Certificate in Human Resource Management
    • Certificate in Business Management
    • Certificate in Cooperative Management

    Mathenge Technical Training Institute fees

    The institute has not updated its fee structure for the 2024/2025 intake as of this writing. It is vital to note that the annual fees vary based on the courses and other admission charges. Also, looking at the September 2023 fee structure, the institution has an average yearly fee of Ksh.68,890 that cuts across all programs.

    New students looking to enrol are advised to check for a detailed Mathenge Technical Training Institute fees structure before starting the application process. They can download an admission letter with the termly and annual fees charged for their preferred courses or visiting the school's official website.

    Mathenge Technical Training Institute intake requirements

    The intake requirements vary depending on the training levels students choose. Here are the three primary levels and their minimum admission requirements:

    • Artisan programs: Applicants must have completed secondary school and have a KCSE certificate.
    • Certificated courses: Minimum grade of D+ in KCSE and hold an artisan certificate from a recognized institution or their equivalent.
    • Diploma courses: Minimum grade of C- in KCSE and hold a craft certificate from a recognized institute or their equivalent.

    Mathenge Technical Training Institute application process

    Mathenge TTI spells out its straightforward application process on its website. The process starts by downloading and filling out the Mathenge Technical Training Institute admission letter and providing other necessities.

    As a KUCCPS student, you should have the following requirements before starting the application process:

    • A valid email address
    • A valid telephone number registered under your name
    • KCPE and KCSE index numbers and years of examination
    • Passport size photo
    • College or university admission letter
    • Birth certificate
    • Parents' ID numbers or death certificates of any of your deceased parent.
    • Copy of your national ID
    • Two guarantors' ID numbers

    Once you have all the requirements ready, here is how you can apply and get enrolled in your preferred program:

  • Visit the official institution's website, click "Admission", and select the "KUCCPS student placement and admission letters" option.
  • Click on the "Download Admission Letter Here" button at the bottom of the page, which will take you to the download page.
  • Search for your course and download the letter using the download button on the right.
  • You can also search for specific course requirements using the search menu on the page. This will take you to your KUCCPS students' portal.
  • Fill out the admission letter with the necessary details and submit it online or physically by visiting the institution in person.

    How many Mathenge Technical Training Institute hostels are there?

    As a growing institution, Mathenge has worked continuously to improve its students' accommodation and general welfare. Currently, the school has day and boarding students. It also has separate accommodation for its male and female students. Moreover, there is an allocated head for each hostel who ensures students adhere to the accommodation rules.

    Where is Mathenge Technical Training Institute located?

    Mathenge Technical Training Institute is located in the Mahiga neighbourhood, Othaya constituency, Nyeri County. Its strategic location, 12 kilometres from Othaya town and 18 kilometres from Nyeri town, allows easy access from both directions.
