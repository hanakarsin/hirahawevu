    A Baringo househelp who is accused of killing a police officer's seven-year-old son and injuring his wife has finally been arrested weeks after the incident.

    Lydiah Jelagat Kipng'etich, 24, who had been offered a househelp job in the home of a Rumuruti-based prison warded, attacked the family on the night of September 24, 2021, and committed the atrocities.

    According to a statement from the Directorate of Criminal Investigations, a panga-wielding Jelagat crept into the couple's bedroom on the fateful night and attacked the officer's wife.
