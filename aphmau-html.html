    YouTuber gamer and Twitch streamer named Jess who primarily plays Minecraft. She has played other games for her channel that include Pixelmon, The Sims 4 and Spore. She and her husband later became the founders of the company CatFace.

    Before Fame

    Launching her YouTube channel on August 13, 2012, she began her career with a Minecraft Tekkit series and a Saints Row the Third series.

    Trivia

    She has over 13 million subscribers on her YouTube channel. The most popular videos on her channel are her Minecraft Diaries series. Several of her videos, including "The New House | Minecraft MyStreet [Ep.1 Minecraft Roleplay]," have earned over 5 million views. 

    Family Life

    She was born Jessica Bravura in Houston, Texas and grew up alongside her older brother. Her husband's name is Jason; they have children named Joseph, Julia, Jennifer, and Jessabelle.

    Associated With

    She is a player of Minecraft, a game which was developed by Markus Persson.
