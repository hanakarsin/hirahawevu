    The gruesome murder of a female preacher who was hacked to the death in Abuja has generated outrage among some Nigerians.

    The 42 year-old mother of seven, Mrs. Eunice Elisha, was a deaconess at the Divine Touch parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, (RCCG) Old NEPA Road, Phase 4, Kubwa. She had gone out to evangelize along Gbazango-West area of Kubwa on Saturday moring, July 9, when she was hacked to death by unknown assailants suspected to be extremists.

    The husband of the deceased, Pastor Olawale Elisha who is still in shock over the murder of his wife told journalists that his wife had gone out to preach in the neighbourhood at about 5 am only for him to hear the report of her murder.
