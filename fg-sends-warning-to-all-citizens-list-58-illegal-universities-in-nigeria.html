    FG Sends Warning To All Citizens, List 58 Illegal Universities In Nigeria

    National University Commission(NUC) has stated on its website that the federal government has shut down nothing less than 58 universities across Nigeria. The universities were closed for disobeying the Education (National Minimum Standards, etc.) Act CAPE3 Law of the Federation of Nigeria 2004.

    08 Apr 2024, 00:00

    The federal government of Nigeria has sent warning to all Nigerians especially those planning on attending  Universities by sending a list of illegal institution.

    National University Commission(NUC) has stated on its website that the federal government has shut down nothing less than 58 universities across Nigeria.

    The universities were closed for disobeying the Education (National Minimum Standards, etc.) Act CAPE3 Law of the Federation of Nigeria 2004.

    The federal government sent the message through the NUC secretary to all citizens, undergraduate and prospective university students.
