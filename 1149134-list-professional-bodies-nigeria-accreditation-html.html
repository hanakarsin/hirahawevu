    Professional bodies and institutions are established for regulatory purposes. They decide, maintain, and regulate the ethics of practice and conduct of professionals in their respective profession. Below is a list of professional bodies in Nigeria with accreditation.

    There are many professional bodies in Nigeria today. Professions like accounting, engineering, banking, surveyor, medicine, etc. all have professional bodies, associations, and institutions in Nigeria. Being a member of a professional body in your profession is very important. It advances your career and opens you up to a lot of opportunities to get to the peak level of your career in Nigeria.

    Some of the professional bodies have short courses which award professional certificate to its members. These certificates are rated highly by employers and for executive positions in Nigeria. Below is a list of some of the professional bodies in Nigeria with accreditation.

    This is the list of professional bodies in Nigeria with their office address and website. It might not contain all of such bodies but it has some of the most important and popular ones.
