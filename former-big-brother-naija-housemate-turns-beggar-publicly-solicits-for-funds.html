    Former Big Brother Naija Housemate Turns 'Beggar', Publicly Solicits For Funds

    The reality star who decided to quit music months after her appearance on the show in 2019, posted her bank details via her verified Instagram page on Thursday. According to her, she wasnt going to stop until her helper finds her.

    29 May 2024, 00:00

    Former BBNaija housemate Ella Nnabuchi has unashamedly sought out for funds from the general public following the economic hardship in the country.

    The reality star who decided to quit music months after her appearance on the show in 2019, posted her bank details via her verified Instagram page on Thursday.

    According to her, she wasn’t going to stop until her helper finds her.

    Speaking about the reason behind her decision to solicit for funds publicly, the reality star hinted that, her brand needs rebranding, hence the urgent need for money.

    In her words: ”Not Going to stop posting my details until I find my helper oooo. God abeg so nobody love me like this. All you coming to say I need rebranding is it my teeth I will use to support.”

    Phyna Speaks About Her ‘Adopted’ Daughter

    Meanwhile, the winner of the BBNaija Season 7 reality show, Phyna, has shared more details concerning her daughter.

    Phyna, while speaking during an interview on E-Splash podcast, disclosed that she has not seen her child since she left the house.

    During the chat, one of the interviewers asked the self-acclaimed Hype Priestess about her child.

    Responding, the reality TV star said that her daughter is fine but she has not met her since she left the house.

    She went on to state that the girl in question is not her biological daughter but her elder cousin’s child whose wife died while she was pregnant and an urgent cesarean operation had to be carried out to bring out the child.

    Speaking further, Phyna explained that she has been taking care of the girl since she was born and therefore describes her as her child.
