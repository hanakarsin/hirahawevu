    Gov. Wike Is A Dictator

    Governor Nyesom Wike of Rivers State has been described as a dictator by Ebonyi State Governor, Dave Umahi. Umahi who recently joined the All Progressives Congress during an interview with Arise TV, accused Wike of single-handedly controlling the affairs of the Peoples Democratic Party.

    28 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Governor Nyesom Wike of Rivers State has been described as a dictator by Ebonyi State Governor, Dave Umahi.

    Umahi who recently joined the All Progressives Congress during an interview with Arise TV, accused Wike of single-handedly controlling the affairs of the Peoples Democratic Party.

    He further stated that his actions has made governors under the party unhappy with him.

    “Wike must know he is a dictator; he must know that one person cannot be called a crowd.”

    “Wike must know that a lot of governors in the PDP are very sad with him,” Umahi added

    He added that Wike single-handedly made Secondus the PDP Chairman and tried to impose one of his own as the minority leader in the House of Representatives.

    “He must know that there is nothing that happens in the PDP without his authorization.”

    Umahi called on the Rivers State Governor not to clash with him as such will be dangerous.

    The Ebonyi State Governor charged Wike to focus on the party and stop demonstrating dictatorship traits by attacking him.

    “Wike has called the PDP committee all kinds of unprintable names, and I have asked Gov. Wike, he is my friend, and let’s not take on ourselves because it will be very dangerous.”

    “Focus on the party, the party does not belong to him and this is nothing personal but Wike is demonstrating the dictatorship tendencies in him by taking on me a fellow governor.”
